Interests

As an biologist and embryologist, I regularly encounter mass data sets containing vast amounts of information gathered from experiments & patient outcomes. We gather huge amounts of data which could provide great insights to improve patient outcomes. However, we lack an ability to fully interrogate this information & draw meaningful conclusions. By learning data science techniques, I aim to leverage that knowledge along with my research experience as a biologist/embryologist to investigate our clinical data sets & find ways we can improve patient outcomes. Long-term I aim to return to the biotech space as a data scientist where I hope to apply my skills to biological data sets.

Apart from my passion in the sciences, I also enjoy the outdoors. I taught myself to draw stick-shift on the icy roads of Iceland, and I'm an avid skier. I also have an interest in languages and food, and can speak and read French.
